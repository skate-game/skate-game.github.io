The Playbook: Step-by-Step

1. Account & Identity

Register your account in seconds. You'll set a unique Skater Tag—this is your calling card. Use the search bar to find friends by their tags and send out your challenges.

2. One Shot (The 10-Second Rule)

When it's your turn, open your match. Hit record, and the camera is live. You get exactly 10 seconds to land your trick. There are no retries, no editing, and no "practice runs." The clip uploads automatically the moment the time is up.

3. The Verdict

Once a trick is posted, your opponent watches the raw footage. They vote either Clean or Bailed. You do the same for their tricks.

4. Handling Deadlocks

If you disagree on whether a trick was landed, the app enters a Deadlock state. A 10-second timer starts; both players must reach a consensus. If the timer hits zero without an agreement, the "Skate Gods" intervene, clear the votes, and apply a strike against your deadlock count (3 strikes = automatic forfeit).

Core Features

No Retakes

The recording system is final. Once you hit record, that's your turn. It keeps the pressure high and the game honest.

Trash Talk Included

Use the built-in match chat to discuss the trick, hype each other up, or trade lighthearted jabs while you wait for the next turn.

Built for the Community

This is a passion project currently under development. There are no ads, no pay-to-play features, and no corporate nonsense. It’s just a tool for skaters, built by a skater.
